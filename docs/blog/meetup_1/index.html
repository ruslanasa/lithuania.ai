Artificial Intelligence Meetup #1

Oct 10, 2019 | 1 minute read
Share this: Twitter Facebook Pinterest Google+

Artificial Intelligence Group has grown up into something much bigger. From now on all the previous AI efforts will be combined under one new flag of “Artificial Intelligence Association of Lithuania”. Join our first official meetup to learn more about the organisation and how to become a part of it.

Agenda

  • Intro - Audrius Zujus (President @ Artificial Intelligence Association of Lithuania)
  • “Deploy your neural net: engineering platform agnostic inference." - Renaldas Zioma (Shepherd at Unity)
  • “Transforming retail using AI” - Mindaugas Eglinskas (CEO @ Pixevia)
  • “Artificial Intelligence Association of Lithuania” - Audrius Zujus (President @ Artificial Intelligence Association of Lithuania)
