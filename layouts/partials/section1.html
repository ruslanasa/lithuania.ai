Mission

What are we doing?

We gather the community of AI of Lithuania to debate about AI technology, find the best interpretation of legal AI issues as well as education of public on AI topics. We realize the potential of an emerging sector of AI and welcome participants from all sectors to create a better environment to do fundamental research, explore and encourage AI startups as well as AI adoption in business. Most important we pursue sustainable and effective AI integration to all sectors of the ecosystem.

Vision

What are we trying to achieve?

We strive to connect and lead the AI community of Lithuania to become strong and competitive within Europe and the global context. We pursue this by campaign partnerships between the academic and private sectors, as well as government and public sectors. We are formulating and expressing guidelines for national AI strategy, including the ethics, legal questions of AI. This joint-venture, putting together the best from all sectors, can contribute to the fundamental research in AI and the innovation ecosystem, being very beneficial to the whole country.
